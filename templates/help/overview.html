SilentCanary Overview

Welcome to SilentCanary! A modern, intelligent monitoring solution that keeps track of your critical processes, scheduled jobs, and services through simple HTTP check-ins.

What is SilentCanary?

SilentCanary is a dead man's switch monitoring system that ensures your critical processes are running by expecting regular "check-ins" from them. If a process fails to check in within its expected timeframe, SilentCanary immediately alerts you.

Process Monitoring

Monitor cron jobs, background processes, scheduled tasks, and services.

Smart Alerts

ML-powered anomaly detection that learns your patterns and reduces false alarms.

Multi-Channel Alerts

Email and Slack notifications with rich context and actionable information.

Quick Example:
# In your backup script
#!/bin/bash
# ... your backup logic ...

# Notify SilentCanary that backup completed
curl -X POST "https://your-domain.com/checkin/abc123def456"

If your backup script fails or doesn't run, SilentCanary will alert you within the specified grace period.

Key Features

  • Simple HTTP Check-ins: Just GET/POST to a URL
  • Flexible Intervals: Minutes to days
  • Grace Periods: Account for expected variations
  • Tags: Organize your canaries
  • SLA Monitoring: Track uptime percentages
  • Smart Alerts: ML-based anomaly detection
  • Multi-Channel Notifications: Email + Slack
  • Analytics: Detailed uptime reports
  • Export Data: CSV/JSON exports
  • API Access: Programmatic management
